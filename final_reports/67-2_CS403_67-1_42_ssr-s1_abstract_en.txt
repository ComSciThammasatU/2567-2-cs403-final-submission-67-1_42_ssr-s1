This special project is to study data and guidelines for creating a management system, taking care of and managing logistics data to help promote efficiency in business transportation, which involves transporting goods or resources over land using vehicles such as cars that require transportation routes and certain actions may cause risks or specific events that can damage safety or business assets. 
During the implementation of this project, a system for tracking and managing vehicle data within the transportation company was studied and developed, enabling an understanding of the operation and importance of various aspects of data that must be defined and transmitted through vehicles for processing specific events within the system.
